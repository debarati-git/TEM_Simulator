    INSTRUMENT OVERVIEW
    JEOL JEM-2100
    The JEM-2100 is a 200 kV transmission electron microscope built for high-resolution imaging and selected-area electron diffraction (SAED). It features a LaB₆ thermionic electron gun, a five-lens condenser–objective system, and a bottom-mounted CCD camera.

    The instrument achieves a point resolution of 0.23 nm and a lattice resolution of 0.14 nm, making it well suited for nanoparticle characterisation, grain-boundary analysis, and crystallographic studies.
