Stage 01 · The Column

Inside a TEM Column

1
Hover the column labels
Each component opens an inspector explaining its function — work top to bottom: gun → condenser → objective → projector.
2
Drag the voltage slider
Watch the de Broglie wavelength shrink in real time and compare it against the Si lattice spacing reference line.
3
Tap ϕ for equations
Opens the full formula — de Broglie wavelength, Bragg's law, or point resolution — with variable definitions.
Stage 03 · Diffraction Lab

Real ↔ Reciprocal Space

1
Drag the crystal to rotate
Spin the lattice in 3D and watch the SAED pattern on the right update live to match the new orientation.
2
Pick a crystal & zone axis
Compare patterns from FCC, BCC, and diamond cubic structures along [001], [011], and [111] zone axes.
3
Click any spot to index it
Reveals the (hkl) indices, d-spacing, and Bragg angle. Use the Ruler tool to measure the distance between two spots.
